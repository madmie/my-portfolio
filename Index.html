Portfolio

Workshop 1

Advances in information technology have made digital systems essential for efficient clinic operations. Yet many facial treatment clinics in Malaysia still record client details manually, causing delays, inefficiency, and risk of data loss. To solve these issues, the FacialCare Treatment System was developed. It streamlines clinic workflows by managing client and therapist information, treatment history, and packages. The system allows staff to easily add, update, delete, view, and search records, improving overall accuracy and efficiency..

HCI Project

Advancements in educational technology have enabled more interactive learning, with Augmented Reality (AR) becoming a powerful tool to enhance traditional materials. This project develops an AR E-Book about the Solar System—covering the Sun, inner planets, and outer planets—using 3D visuals, interactive elements, and a user-friendly design to improve understanding and engagement. The Solar System was selected for its importance in science education and suitability for AR, helping learners better connect theoretical concepts with real-world visualization..
